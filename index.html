The Partnership Structure

Defining roles, ownership, and incentives.

Role: Technical Partner

  • • Executes all software development (WordPress, PWA, Integrations).
  • • Fabricates and programs custom ESP32 hardware.
  • • Maintains the "Developer" relationship with Toast.
  • • Retains Intellectual Property (IP) Rights.

Role: Strategic Investor

  • • Provides seed funding for hardware and dev time.
  • • Provides the "Sandbox" (the 3 restaurants) for beta testing.
  • • Offers operational insight for feature requirements.
  • • Receives % of Gross Sales from SaaS Product.
